# Director of Platform Engineering - Chatham Financial

## Role Overview

Leading the strategic planning, development, and management of Chatham Financial's cloud infrastructure. This role involves overseeing the design, implementation, and maintenance of cloud-based systems to ensure optimal performance, security, and scalability. The Director collaborates with cross-functional teams to align cloud strategies with business objectives and drive innovation in cloud technologies.

## Key Responsibilities

### Strategic Planning
Develop and execute a comprehensive cloud infrastructure strategy that supports the company's business goals and technological needs.

### Leadership & Team Development
- Lead and mentor a team of cloud engineers and architects
- Foster a culture of collaboration, innovation, and continuous improvement
- Lead effective performance management and professional talent development across all respective departments

### Cloud Architecture & Implementation
- Design and implement scalable, secure, and cost-effective cloud solutions
- Ensure high availability and disaster recovery capabilities
- Expertise in cloud infrastructure (Azure preferred), Container Orchestration, Terraform, CI/CD, and observability

### Vendor Management
- Manage relationships with Managed Services Providers, Cloud Service Providers, and other key vendor partners
- Build and maintain relationships with key vendors
- Negotiate contracts and create cost-effective agreements
- Ensure service level agreements (SLAs) are met

### Security & Compliance
- Implement robust security measures to protect cloud infrastructure from threats
- Ensure compliance with industry standards and regulations
- Preferred: Experience delivering platform capabilities in a regulated industry or financial services context
- Ideally with SOC1 & SOC2 compliance expertise

### Performance & Reliability
- Monitor cloud infrastructure performance
- Identify and resolve issues to maintain optimal system functionality
- Strong understanding of site reliability principles, SLAs/SLIs/SLOs, and operational excellence

### Budget Management
- Develop and manage the cloud infrastructure budget
- Optimize costs while delivering high-quality services

### Innovation & Technology Leadership
- Stay abreast of emerging cloud technologies and trends
- Recommend and implement innovative solutions to enhance the company's cloud capabilities
- Drive adoption of best practices and modern cloud-native approaches

### Collaboration & Integration
- Work closely with other IT leaders and business stakeholders
- Integrate cloud strategies with overall IT and business plans
- Ensure alignment between technical solutions and business objectives

## Technical Expertise

### Cloud Platforms
- **Extensive knowledge** in Azure Cloud platform
- Strong knowledge of cloud architecture, security, and best practices

### Infrastructure & Tools
- Cloud infrastructure management (Azure preferred)
- Container Orchestration (Kubernetes, AKS)
- Infrastructure as Code (Terraform)
- CI/CD pipelines and automation
- Observability and monitoring solutions

### Site Reliability Engineering
- Site reliability principles and practices
- SLAs (Service Level Agreements)
- SLIs (Service Level Indicators)
- SLOs (Service Level Objectives)
- Operational excellence frameworks

### Compliance & Governance
- Regulated industry experience (financial services preferred)
- SOC1 compliance expertise
- SOC2 compliance expertise
- Industry standards and regulatory requirements

## Key Success Factors

1. **Strategic Vision**: Ability to align cloud infrastructure with long-term business goals
2. **Technical Excellence**: Deep expertise in Azure cloud platform and modern DevOps practices
3. **Leadership**: Proven ability to build, mentor, and develop high-performing teams
4. **Vendor Relations**: Strong negotiation and relationship management skills
5. **Cost Optimization**: Track record of managing budgets and optimizing cloud spend
6. **Security-First**: Commitment to implementing robust security and compliance measures
7. **Innovation**: Passion for staying current with emerging technologies and driving adoption
8. **Collaboration**: Excellent communication and cross-functional partnership skills

## Impact Areas

### Business Value
- Reduce operational costs through cloud optimization
- Improve system reliability and uptime
- Accelerate time-to-market for new services
- Enable business scalability and growth

### Technical Excellence
- Modernize infrastructure with cloud-native approaches
- Implement automation and self-service capabilities
- Enhance security posture and compliance readiness
- Establish observability and monitoring best practices

### Team Development
- Build a skilled and motivated platform engineering team
- Create a culture of continuous learning and improvement
- Develop career paths and growth opportunities
- Foster innovation and technical excellence

## Alignment with Azure Cloud Migration Project

This role directly supports the Azure Cloud Migration initiative by:

1. **Strategic Leadership**: Driving the overall cloud strategy and migration roadmap
2. **Technical Guidance**: Ensuring architectural decisions align with best practices
3. **Team Enablement**: Building the team capabilities needed for successful migration
4. **Vendor Coordination**: Managing Azure partnership and support relationships
5. **Security & Compliance**: Ensuring migrated workloads meet regulatory requirements
6. **Cost Management**: Optimizing cloud spend during and after migration
7. **Innovation**: Identifying opportunities to modernize applications during migration
8. **Operational Excellence**: Establishing SRE practices for migrated workloads
